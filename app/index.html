Studio

Making Records

Located in the scenic Shenandoah Valley

Poetown Studio offers its clients all the necessary components to make professional recordings – state of art tracking facilities, post-production techniques, and experienced engineers. Hourly rates, day rates, lockouts and lodging are available for all projects large or small.



Are you a songwriter?

Are you a songwriter that would like to have your song published? Poetown Music has a team of professionals that can bring to life your compositions. Armed with a stable of professional studio musicians, and experienced producers Poetown Music can create industry standard demos of your songs ready to pitch to publishing houses, film/TV production house, labels, etc.

Poetown Music also provides songwriting services ranging from co-writing to detailed feedback from the viewpoint of industry professionals. Whether you are a lyricist who would like to have your words put to music, or a lone songwriter looking to market your songs, Poetown Music turn your creativity in reality.

Career Development

Making Careers in Music

Poetown Music specializes in turning who you are into what you do. Whether you need the basics or a little fine-tuning, at Poetown Music we prepare you for a successful and satisfying career in music.

Management

Making Your Way Through the Music Business

At Poetown Music we manage artists who we are truly passionate about. As full-service managers, we assist artists in navigating the challenges and opportunities in today’s changing music industry, using our know-how and passion to take artists “to the next level” and build careers that last.
